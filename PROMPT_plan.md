## Your Task

Your task is to analyze gaps between the specifications in `specs/*` and the current codebase (`src/*` and `src/lib/*`), then generate a prioritized task list in `IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`.

## Process

1. Read all specification files in `specs/*.md` to understand what needs to be built.
2. Explore the current codebase to understand existing structure and what has already been implemented.
3. Identify what's missing or needs updating.
4. Create or update `IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md` with prioritized tasks.
5. Commit your changes.

## Important

- Do NOT implement anything in this mode — only plan.
- Be thorough in your analysis.
- Prioritize tasks based on dependencies (things that others depend on first).
- Keep the task list actionable and specific.
